This is a prototype lens rental application. The backend is powered by Flask, PostgreSQL, Celery, and Redis, and the frontend is powered by Bootstrap 4, Typeahead/Bloodhound, and DataTables. Users can create, retrieve, edit, and delete, catalog of lenses and customers. Lenses and can rented out and returned. Newsletters can also be sent (asynchronously) to subscribed customers.

The name of this application is a reference to Leica, a German Company that manufactures optics and high-end cameras. Leica is one of my favorite lens makers for 35mm lenses. I've pre-populated the catalog with a few of their well-known LTM and M lenses. I've taken the banner image from their website (no copyright infringement intended).
